Ubud Rice Terraces & Waterfall Day Trip

Bali, Indonesia

4.9 (3,789 reviews)
8 hrs Duration
Max 8 Group size
EN / ID Languages
Free Cancellation up to 24h

About this experience

Trade the beach for the highlands on this small-group day trip through Bali's rice country, led by a host who grew up walking these same terrace paths.

Cool off under a jungle waterfall, share a home-style lunch, and end the day wandering Ubud's temples and markets before the drive back.

Highlights

  • The iconic Tegalalang rice terraces at golden hour
  • Swim (or just wade) at a jungle waterfall
  • Home-style Balinese lunch with a local family
  • Free time in Ubud's temples, markets and monkey forest area
  • Small group of 8 travelers max — no big-bus crowds

What's included

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off
  • Air-conditioned vehicle
  • Local host and driver
  • Lunch and bottled water
  • Entrance fee to Monkey Forest (optional, ~$10)
  • Alcoholic drinks
  • Gratuities (optional)

Itinerary
